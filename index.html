CHOOSE YOUR ADVENTURE

Three ways to fish the Florida Keys. Pick your style, pick your time, and Captain Willy handles the rest.

TROLLING & LIVE BAIT

Troll the deep blue or free-line live bait across the reef edge — whatever's biting, we're on 'em. Pelagic giants on light or heavy tackle, acrobatic jumps, lightning runs.

  • Mahi-mahi — fast action, great dinner
  • Sailfish — acrobatic jumps, catch & release
  • Wahoo — blistering speed, razor teeth
  • Marlin — the trophy of a lifetime
  • Tuna — hard-fighting, sushi-grade

SHIPWRECK FISHING

Drop baits on Key West's legendary wrecks and reefs for bottom-dwelling bruisers. These structures hold massive fish year-round.

  • Gag & black grouper — reef giants that test your arms
  • Snapper — mutton, red, mangrove, lane
  • Amberjack, cobia, barracuda — wrecks hold 'em all
